How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich


Sometimes you don't have a lot of time and want to make something simple, the classic peanut butter jelly sandwich has your back.


In order to make a peanut butter jelly sandwich, you are going to want some bread, a jar of peanut butter, and a jar of jelly or jam.


Get two slices of bread and a butter knife and spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread, and the jam on another.

Optionally, you can also toast the bread before this step to have a toasted PB&J Sandwich.


Put the slices of bread together, and there you go. You now have a peanut butter and jelly sandwich. It's that easy.
