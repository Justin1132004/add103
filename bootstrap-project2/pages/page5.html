How to make Egg Toast/Egg in the Hole


Quite easy to make, Egg in the Hole is a nice combination of eggs and toast all in one food item. It can be a fun meal to make, or easily prepared in a hurry.


You'll need a piece of toast and an egg. Use something to drill a hole in the center of the toast, I use the top of a cup or mug. After the center is removed, crack the egg and pour the contents into the bread.


Cook on medium heat in a pan until one side of the egg is done then flip the bread. - Careful, I've messed this up before! Cook the other side, and add pepper or other seasoning for flavor.


And now you have a fairly simple 5 minute breakfast with a wholesome flavor.
