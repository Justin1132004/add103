How to use a drip coffee machine


If there is one thing I can't go without in the morning, it is coffee. This is how you can make a batch in the morning.


First, remove the filter basket from the machine and dump out any used filters if they are present. Wash and dry out the basket before putting in a fresh filter.


Put ground coffee grinds into the filter, adjust depending on how many cups you are making. I use 2 and a half spoonfuls for two people each morning.
Put the filter basket back into the machine.


Remove the water carafe from the machine and fill it with water, I fill it up to 6 for two people. Open the machine's water tank and pour the water from the carafe inside, then put the carafe back onto the heating plate.
Turn on the machine, and it will begin to pour your coffee.
This process can take up to ten minutes, so if you in a major hurry and there is a previous batch in the carafe then just turning on the machine to heat it up on the heating plate does the trick, however it won't be as good as a fresh batch.
